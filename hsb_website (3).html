About the HSB
The Homeland Security Bureau is the top-tier federal law enforcement agency in Paradise State Roleplay. Established to combat the most dangerous criminal organizations and threats, the HSB operates with authority that transcends local jurisdiction.
Our agents undergo rigorous training and are held to the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and excellence. We work alongside local law enforcement while maintaining federal oversight on matters of national security and organized crime across all of San Andreas.
Our Divisions
Homeland Investigations Command
Handles major criminal investigations, organized crime, and high-profile cases across San Andreas. HIC agents operate undercover and in the field to dismantle criminal networks threatening the state.
Special Response Unit
HSB's elite tactical arm deployed for high-risk operations including armed standoffs, hostage rescue, counter-terrorism raids, and VIP protection across all of San Andreas.
Immigration & Customs Agency
Enforces federal immigration law, investigates customs violations, and secures the borders of San Andreas. ICA officers work ports of entry, airports, and coordinate with international agencies.
High-Speed Unit
Specialized pursuit and rapid response division. HSU operators are trained in high-speed vehicle interdiction, emergency pursuit driving, and rapid deployment across all terrains of San Andreas.
Join the HSB
Think you have what it takes to serve San Andreas at a federal level? Review the requirements and submit your application through our Discord.
Requirements
Must be an active member of Paradise State Roleplay (PSRP)
Minimum age of 14 years old — maturity and professionalism required
No active disciplinary actions or bans within PSRP
Ability to follow chain of command and standard HSB protocols
Willingness to complete HSB Academy training before active deployment
Must remain active on Discord and in-server regularly
Application Process
1
Submit Your Application
Complete the HSB application form in the designated channel on our Discord server.
2
Interview
Shortlisted applicants will be contacted for a brief interview with an HSB Command officer.
3
Academy Training
Accepted recruits complete HSB Academy training covering procedures, tactics, and field protocols.
4
Field Assignment
Upon graduation you receive your callsign, rank, and division and begin active operations.
